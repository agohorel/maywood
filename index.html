About us:

Our mission is to preserve the history of Sidney Center and strengthen the connection between residents and their heritage through the collection, curation, and presentation of archives, exhibits, and community events that highlight the significance of our local history.

Come visit us in the museam or at one of our weekly ice cream socials. Live music and delicious ice cream!
